What is CGPA?

CGPA is the average of grade points obtained in all semesters.

How to boost CGPA?

  • Focus on high credit subjects
  • Improve weak grades
  • Consistent performance

Formula

CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits
