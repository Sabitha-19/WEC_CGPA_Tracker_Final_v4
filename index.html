CGPA Info & Tips

CGPA: Cumulative Grade Point Average is the weighted average of your semester GPAs.

How to Boost CGPA:

  • Focus on improving grades in high-credit courses.
  • Retake low scored courses if allowed.
  • Practice past papers and stay consistent.
  • Use time management to maximize efficiency.
