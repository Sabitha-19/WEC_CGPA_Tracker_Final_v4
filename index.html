What is CGPA?

CGPA represents your overall academic performance across semesters.

Formula

CGPA = Total GPA ÷ Number of Semesters

How to Boost CGPA?

  • Focus on high-credit subjects
  • Score well in internals
  • Never skip assignments
  • Stay consistent and motivated
