About CGPA

What is CGPA? CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted average of your semester GPA scores.

Formula: CGPA = (Sum of all semester GPA × credits) ÷ Total credits

Tips to boost CGPA:

  • Focus on high credit subjects.
  • Maintain consistent grades each semester.
  • Use internal marks effectively.

FAQs:

Q: Can I recalculate after changing a grade?
A: Yes, just update grades and recalc.
