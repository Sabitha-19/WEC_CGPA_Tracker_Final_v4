What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all semester grade points. Keep track of your scores to improve your overall performance.

Tips to Boost CGPA

  • Focus on weaker subjects first.
  • Practice previous year question papers.
  • Attend all classes and labs.
  • Ask for guidance from seniors/mentors.
