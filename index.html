What is CGPA?

CGPA is the average of grade points obtained across all semesters.

How to Boost CGPA?

  • Focus on high-credit subjects
  • Improve weak grades
  • Maintain consistency

Formula

CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits
