Overview of CGPA

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted average of your semester GPAs. It helps track your overall academic performance.

How to Boost Your CGPA

  • Focus on subjects with higher credits.
  • Improve grades in remaining semesters.
  • Practice previous exams and assignments.
  • Seek help from teachers and peers.
